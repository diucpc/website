    Overview

    DIU-CSE-CPC was founded in July 28, 2019 by honorable chairman Abdul Baset sir, our honorable mentor Samrat Kumar Dey, Jilani Khandaker, Abu Sayed.

    DIU-CSE-CPC officially started working from September 01, 2019 by opening member registration form and publishing this site.

    The main purposes of this club are-

    1. Organizing programming contest
    2. Designing and developing project
    3. Organizing seminar and workshop
    4. Motivate members to build things
    5. Building personality by teamwork
    6. Helping members to develope career
    7. Researching on various topic
    8. Working with opensource network
